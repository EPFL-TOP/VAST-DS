Pick an experiment, enter your annotator name, press Load queue. You'll get one somite tile at a time (exactly the crop the future severity classifier will see) plus the full straightened well for spatial context, with the current somite outlined in red. Pick a severity (0=healthy · 1=mild · 2=moderate · 3=severe) or Unsure; Skip moves on without saving. Labels are written to the SomiteAnnotation table, keyed by (dest_well, somite_index, annotator) so multiple raters can co-exist. Re-loading skips tiles you've already labelled.
