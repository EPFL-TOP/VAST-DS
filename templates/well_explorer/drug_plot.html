Select a drug derivation and one or more experiments, then click Update plot. The page shows three views of the same data, all sharing the validity and annotation-subset filters in the left panel:

  • Dose-response — mean somite count (± SEM) at each Drug.concentration for the selected derivation, log x-axis. Single-concentration drugs collapse to a single point with error bars. Blue = predicted, orange = annotated; click a legend entry to hide either series.
  • Histograms — distributions of total / defective somite counts across all selected wells, predicted vs annotated overlaid.
  • Predicted vs annotated — per-well scatter, coloured by which subset (train / val / test) the manual annotation belongs to. Useful for spotting systematic over/under-prediction.
