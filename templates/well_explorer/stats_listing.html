Prediction vs annotation comparison ? Restricted to valid annotations only (valid=True) — invalid fish have unreliable somite counts and are excluded from analysis.

Each metric is split by the subset the annotated well was assigned to, using the use_for_training / use_for_validation / use_for_test flags on DestWellProperties:

Train: wells the model was trained on. MAE here is biased low — don't trust it as generalisation error.
Val: wells used for early stopping during training. Slightly biased low.
Test: held-out, never seen during training — this is the only honest generalisation metric.
All: combined — useful for sanity checking only.

Annotations with none of the three flags set are excluded (we don't know which subset they belong to).

MAE (Mean Absolute Error) on somite counts, typical range 3–7:
<3 → excellent · 3–7 → good · >7 → consider retraining.
